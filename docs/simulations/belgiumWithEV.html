Analysis: What Does This Simulation Prove?

What this proves: In Belgium, a single 15-minute period of 11kW EV charging creates an 11kW peak that affects your capacity fee for the entire year. This simulation shows a typical Belgian household with occasional evening EV charging sessions. Without intervention, these EV charging peaks become your monthly capacity, potentially costing an extra €300-400/year compared to a lower baseline. With a home battery, these peaks can be shaved by discharging stored energy during EV charging. This demonstrates why peak shaving is especially valuable for Belgian EV owners - limiting charging peaks directly reduces your annual capacity fee.
